1. Is Hari Raya only celebrated by Muslims?

    2. Yes, Hari Raya is primarily celebrated by Muslims as a religious festival marking the end of Ramadan.

  2. What is the significance of fasting during Ramadan?

    3. Fasting during Ramadan is a pillar of Islam and is observed to purify the soul, practice self-discipline, and empathize with those less fortunate.

  3. How long does Hari Raya last?

    4. Hari Raya is typically celebrated for one to three days, depending on cultural and regional practices.

  4. Are non-Muslims allowed to participate in Hari Raya celebrations?

    5. While Hari Raya is primarily a Muslim festival, non-Muslim friends and neighbors are often welcome to join in the festivities as a gesture of inclusivity and harmony.

  5. What are some traditional greetings exchanged during Hari Raya?

    6. Common greetings include "Selamat Hari Raya" (Happy Hari Raya) and "Maaf Zahir dan Batin" (Forgive me, both physically and spiritually).
